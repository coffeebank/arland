Arland Server

A fun, casual community for all!

​Minecraft 1.10 Survival server
Hi there!
We're Arland, a friendly little Survival server. We have a bunch of cool, fun plugins with great enhancements that make playing a normal vanilla Minecraft game boring! Click here to learn more about us >
Our Story
We are currently a 3-man team running a server and a website.
Because we only have 3 staff members, we're looking to recruit more staff!
If you'd like to check us out, our site is at http://site.arland.me and our server IP is play.arland.me
Who are you recruiting?
We're looking to recruit builders and testers for Arland! At the moment, we do not need Mods or Devs yet.
Builders - We need help building structures and terrain, and decorating the server.
Testers - We need users to help us beta test our latest updates, programs, and apps!
How do I apply?
All the information you need to apply is available by clicking the giant button below!
