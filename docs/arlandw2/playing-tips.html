Wand, Skills, and Magic

8/16/2016

0 Comments

 
As you play, you may notice that mobs will increase in level the farther you get from spawn, granting them additional health and damage. How would you combat this? 

If you checked your inventory, you may have noticed a stick named “Wand.” This by itself does nothing, but you can assign Skill Points (SP) to get skills in the skill menu shown /skills. Hover over a skill to see information about it, and click to get it. On the hover menu, you will see click combos to use the skill and information about it. 
You can also assign Attribute Points (AP) to boost your strength , health, mana generation, and a few other things. Type /attr to open up your attribute menu where you can spend your AP. 
By killing monsters, you will level up and gain more AP and SP which you can use to fight higher level monsters easier.

For beginners, I highly recommend at least one point into "Magic Armor."
