Help Support Arland!

Arland is a free Minecraft server for everyone to play. While it may be free for you to play, we still have to pay for hosting, employees, and other fees.

Instead of forcing these fees onto you, we decided to trust the good in everyone's hearts to donate whatever they can to help keep Arland running for everyone! Any help is appreciated, so donate whatever you can -- whether if it's 1¢ or $100!
