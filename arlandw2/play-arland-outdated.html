Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arland?
Arland is a Minecraft server with plenty of fun add-ons that make playing Arland unique and creative. Come join us, it's free! :D
How much does it cost to play Arland?
Arland is free to play for everyone, as long as you have Minecraft.
What version of Minecraft do I need?
Arland can run on any Minecraft 1.9 and 1.10 versions. (This includes all the numbers in between, for example 1.9.4)
Is Arland Survival or Creative?
Both! The main server is in survival mode. However, we also have another world called Blackreach, which is in creative mode. Play in any world you want!
