· What is Arland? ·

Arland is a Minecraft 1.10 server with fun plugins that transform your gaming experience! With plugins such as Essentials, PlayerVaults, and GriefPrevention, building in Minecraft has never been easier!


Arcraft Launcher

Arcraft Launcher takes playing Arland to the next level with client-side mods and a customized launcher that adds even more cool features -- including access to our mod-filled Arworld server!


Arworld

Arworld is a modded server by Arland Studios only accessible from Arcraft Launcher! Try out all sorts of cool mods, from technology to new monsters!
