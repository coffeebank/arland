Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arland?

Arland is a collection of Minecraft servers with mods and plugins that make playing a normal game of Minecraft much more exciting!

​Arland has two servers:
        -     Arland Lite - has plugins
        -     Arworld - has mods (exclusive to Arcraft Launcher)

Find out more about what plugins and mods Arland has!​​
​

What do I get by downloading Arcraft Launcher?

We recommend Arcraft Launcher for newcomers and players that are new to Minecraft.

Arcraft Launcher packages Arland Lite, Arworld, and normal Minecraft into a single launcher, which makes playing much easier!

What version of Minecraft do I need?

If you downloaded Arcraft Launcher, you're all set! Minecraft will update automatically :D

If you already have Minecraft downloaded, you'll need Minecraft 1.10 in order to play Arland Lite.

Wait... Arland is free to play?

Yes! Arland is F2P (Free to play) for everyone, as long as you have a Minecraft account! But if you'd like to donate, send us some money here!

If you do not have a Minecraft account, you can check out our guide here.
