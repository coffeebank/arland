Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arland?
Arland is a Minecraft server with plenty of fun add-ons that make playing Arland unique and creative.

We offer 3 play modes:
  • Arland Lite - for visitors, spectator mode
  • Arland - sign up for free and start playing! 😀
  • Arcraft Launcher - get the full Arcraft experience with mods, also free!
​
How much does it cost to play Arland?
Arland is free to play for everyone, as long as you have a Minecraft account!
What version of Minecraft do I need?

Arland

Arland can run on any Minecraft 1.9 and 1.10 versions. (This includes all the numbers in between, for example 1.9.4)
​

Arcraft Launcher

Arcraft Launcher includes Minecraft and updates automatically, so you don't have to worry about a thing!
​
What makes Arcraft Launcher Different?
With Arland, you need to have Minecraft installed, and then play multiplayer.

However, Arcraft Launcher offers a more seamless experience. When you open Arcraft Launcher, you are given the choice to:
  • Play Arcraft - the full Arcraft experience complete with mods!
  • Play Arland - a shortcut to start playing the original Arland
  • Play Normal Minecraft - play Minecraft normally
​
As you can see, Arcraft Launcher can completely replace Minecraft on your computer. You can do everything straight from Arcraft Launcher!
​
IS ARLAND SURVIVAL OR CREATIVE?
Both! The main server is in survival mode. However, we also have another world called Blackreach, which is in creative mode. Play in any world you want!
