Starting Up Arcraft Launcher

After you finish downloading and installing Arcraft Launcher, just set up a few settings and you're ready to play!
Press OK if these match your current settings.
1. Click "Check for updates" every time you open Arcraft
2. Choose which pack you want to play
​3. Press "Launch" to continue
Login to your account.
Follow on-screen instructions to start playing!
If you do not have a Minecraft account: ​If you have not purchased Minecraft, press Launch, then click Play Offline. Make sure to set your username from the "Account" menu when you finish launching.
